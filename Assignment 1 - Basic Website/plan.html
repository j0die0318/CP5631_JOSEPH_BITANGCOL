Goal

The main goal of the website is to increase patient engagement and appointment bookings at 100SMILES Dental Care by at least 30% over the next six months. This will be achieved by transforming the clinic’s online presence into a modern, user-friendly, and informative platform that reflects the warmth and professionalism of the practice. The site will serve as a central hub for patients to learn about services, meet the team, access educational resources, and take advantage of promotional offers. By improving accessibility, trust, and clarity, the website will help convert casual visitors into loyal patients.

Success Evaluation

  • Email Bookings: Track the number of appointments made via email links on the website using timestamped inquiries.
  • Promotion Engagement: Monitor uptake of “20% off first visit” and “10% off annual check-up” offers via landing pages and email mentions.
  • Article Readership: Track views and time spent on instructional articles using page analytics.
  • Contact Inquiries: Compare pre-launch and post-launch volumes of phone calls and emails initiated from the website.
  • User Feedback: Collect feedback via post-visit surveys or embedded forms focusing on navigation, clarity, and satisfaction.
  • Mobile Responsiveness: Evaluate short session rates and engagement on mobile devices to ensure accessibility across age groups.

Target Audience

Primary Audience:

  • Families with children: Parents seeking trusted dental care for their children.
  • Retirees and older adults: Long-term residents of Razole needing reliable, compassionate dental services.

Design Strategy:

  • Warm and welcoming visuals with friendly imagery and soft color palettes.
  • Simple navigation with large clickable buttons and intuitive page flow.
  • Mobile-friendly layout for smartphones and tablets.
  • Staff introductions with bios and photos to build trust.
  • Service highlights for families and seniors.
  • Readable typography with larger fonts and high-contrast text.
